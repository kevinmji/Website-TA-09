Caution

Caution

Pay attention to the following sections to avoid work accidents

Workplace Safety

Always wear eye, ear, and hand protection when operating the machine. Avoid wearing loose clothing, jewelry, or other items that could get caught in the machine. Ensure the work area is free of flammable materials and obstructions before turning on the machine.

Machine Operation

Do not leave the machine unattended while it is running. Make sure the work material is securely fastened before starting the machining process. Keep hands and other objects away from the cutting area while the machine is in operation.

Maintenance and Care

Turn off and unplug the machine from the power source before performing any maintenance or cleaning. Use only manufacturer-recommended parts and accessories. Conduct regular inspections and promptly replace worn or damaged components to maintain optimal machine performance.

Laser Engrave & CNC Engrave Feature

Never look directly at the laser beam or reflect it off any surface, as it can cause serious eye injury. Ensure the work area is well-ventilated to avoid the buildup of smoke or hazardous gases.Stop the operation immediately if you hear abnormal noises or feel excessive vibrations. Ensure bits and cutting tools are correctly and securely installed before starting the cutting process.

3D Print Feature

Use the printing materials recommended by the manufacturer to avoid damaging the machine. Keep the area around the hot end and the print bed clean to prevent fire hazards or damage.

Installation and Setup

Ensure the machine is installed on a flat and stable surface to prevent excessive vibrations and shifting during operation. Connect the machine to an appropriate power source and make sure all cables are securely and properly connected.

Firmware and Software

Marlin Firmware is a popular open-source firmware for RepRap 3D printers, including many Creality models. It offers a high level of customization and control over various printing functions compared to the stock firmware that comes with your Creality printer. Creality Print (or Creality Slicer) is the slicing software typically provided by Creality for their printers.
